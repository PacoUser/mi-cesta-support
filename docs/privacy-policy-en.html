Privacy Policy - Mi Cesta Pro

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

1. Overview

Mi Cesta Pro is a shopping list application that respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Information we DO NOT collect:

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • We do not access your contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect usage data or analytics

Local information:

  • Your shopping lists are stored locally on your device
  • Scanned products are saved in the local database
  • App settings are maintained on your device

3. iCloud Synchronization

Mi Cesta Pro uses Apple's iCloud to sync your lists across your devices:

  • Data is synchronized through your personal iCloud account
  • Apple manages the encryption and security of this data
  • We have no access to your iCloud information
  • You can disable synchronization at any time

4. Barcode Scanner

When you scan products:

  • The app uses the camera to read barcodes
  • It queries the public Open Food Facts database for product information
  • No camera images are stored or shared
  • Only the barcode is processed, not personal images

5. Voice Recognition

For the voice dictation feature:

  • Voice processing is performed locally on your device
  • No audio is sent to external servers
  • Voice commands are not stored or recorded

6. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. The app works completely offline except for:

  • Optional queries to Open Food Facts for product information
  • Optional iCloud synchronization (managed by Apple)

7. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • iCloud synchronization uses Apple's standard encryption
  • We do not maintain servers with your personal information

8. Children's Privacy

Mi Cesta Pro is safe for all ages. We do not collect personal information from children or any users.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be notified through the App Store and published on this page.
