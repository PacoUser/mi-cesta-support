‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the app theme?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Theme and select one of the 5 professional themes available. Changes apply immediately.
How do I use voice dictation?
Tap the microphone button on the main screen and say the product name. The app will automatically add it to your list.
Does the barcode scanner work offline?
Scanning works offline, but to get detailed product information you need an internet connection. Products are saved locally for future use.
Do my lists sync automatically?
Yes, if you have iCloud enabled, your lists automatically sync across all your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac).
Can I use the app without internet?
Absolutely! Mi Cesta Pro works completely offline. You only need internet for scanning new products and iCloud synchronization.
How do I reorder products in my list?
Long press a product and drag it to the desired position. You can also use the reorder button on each product.
Are the Pro themes worth it?
Pro themes offer a superior visual experience with carefully selected colors, better contrast, and professional design for more comfortable use.

üîß Troubleshooting

Microphone doesn't work

Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Microphone and make sure Mi Cesta Pro has permissions enabled.

Scanner doesn't scan codes

Verify that the camera has permissions in Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Camera. Make sure there's good lighting.

Lists don't sync

Verify that iCloud is enabled in Settings ‚Üí [Your name] ‚Üí iCloud and that you have an internet connection.
